Our Simple Process

We make glass recycling easy, transparent, and rewarding in three simple steps.

1. Drop-Off or Collect

Bring your glass bottles and jars to our yard in Mkondeni or schedule a convenient collection for larger quantities.

2. Weigh & Sort

Our expert team will accurately sort your glass by color and weigh it using certified scales to determine its value.

3. Instant Payment

Receive immediate and fair payment for your recyclables based on current market rates. Quick and hassle-free.
