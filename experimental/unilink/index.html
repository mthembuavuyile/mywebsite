The Problem

A Fragmented Journey

The transition to higher education is broken by silos, duplication, and outdated legacy systems designed for friction.

Application Chaos

Students navigate multiple portals, rebuild their profiles from scratch for each institution, and pay separate admin fees every single time they apply.

Funding Disconnect

Vital bursaries pass students by due to scattered information and inconsistent deadlines.

Physical ID Hassles

Plastic student cards are costly, easily lost, and require standing in hour-long queues.

Isolated Campus Life

Communication, mentorship, and course channels are scattered across fragmented WhatsApp groups and clunky LMS systems that nobody enjoys using.

The Solution

One ecosystem. Every stage of campus life.

unilink is built from the ground up to replace the patchwork of systems students and institutions juggle today — with a single, beautifully integrated platform.

Universal Profile & Apply

Complete your academic details once. Select up to 5 institutions and submit a unified application — data auto-maps to specific criteria (APS, NBT, etc.).

Integrated Funding Hub

Our engine intelligently connects students with NSFAS and corporate bursaries based on merit and demographics. Never miss a deadline.

SA Student Pass

A dynamic, secure, offline-first digital identity QR code. Refreshes cryptographically to prevent fraud, verifying instantly for campus access.

unilink Campus

A unified hub with personal dashboards, threaded course channels, verified peer mentorship, and gamification — replacing fragmented group chats.
