Nexora Voice Assistant

Official User Guide

Welcome to Nexora, your intelligent voice assistant for live data and real-time information, proudly developed by Vylex. Nexora acts as a smart bridge between you and reliable web APIs — fetching current weather, crypto prices, news, dictionary definitions, images, and more through a single, seamless chat interface. Whether you prefer typing or speaking, Nexora is built for speed, accuracy, and accessibility.

AI Limitations & Accuracy

Nexora is designed to be a helpful companion, but like all AI-assisted systems, it may occasionally make mistakes.

  • Verification: Always double-check important factual information, especially for critical decisions, precise quotes, or financial data.
  • Context: The assistant may sometimes misinterpret the nuance or intent behind your voice or text commands.
  • Live Data: Real-time results depend on third-party API availability and accuracy. Nexora is not responsible for errors in external data sources.
  • Continuous Improvement: Nexora is constantly being updated and improved by the Vylex team to reduce errors over time.

Getting Started

Nexora is a web-based application accessible directly through your browser. When you open Nexora, you'll see the main chat interface with a welcome message ready to assist you. Simply type a command or tap the microphone to speak — Nexora will recognise your intent and respond with live data from trusted sources.

Interface Overview

Nexora's interface is designed to be clean and intuitive. Here are the main components:

  • Header: At the top, you'll see the "Nexora" title with a icon. On the right side:
    • New Chat button (): Clears the current conversation and starts fresh.
    • Settings button (): Opens the settings panel to customise your experience.
  • Chat Area: The main part of the screen where your conversation with Nexora takes place.
    • Your messages appear on the right in a light blue bubble.
    • Nexora's responses appear on the left in a light grey bubble.
    • Each message includes a timestamp.
    • When Nexora is processing your request, a typing indicator (three pulsing dots) will appear.
  • Input Area: Located at the bottom.
    • Text Input Field: Type your questions or commands here.
    • Send button (): Click or press Enter to send.
    • Microphone button (): Click to use voice input.

Interacting with Nexora

Typing Messages

  1. Click on the text input field at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Type your question or command (e.g., "Weather in Durban").
  3. Press Enter or click the Send button.

Using Voice Input

  1. Click the Microphone button. Allow browser permission if prompted.
  2. The button will turn red and pulse — Nexora is listening.
  3. Speak your command clearly. Your speech will be transcribed in real-time.
  4. Click the Microphone button again to stop, or let it auto-send when you pause.
Tip: For best voice recognition results, speak clearly and in a relatively quiet environment. You can change the recognition language in the Settings panel.

Customisation & Accessibility

Nexora is designed to adapt to your preferred environment and accessibility needs. Configure the following options using the Settings panel (the gear icon in the header).

Voice Input & Output

Click the microphone icon to speak your requests. You can also enable Read Responses Aloud (Text-to-Speech) and customise the voice type, reading speed, and pitch.

Dictionary Pronunciation

Every dictionary search includes a speaker icon so you can hear the exact pronunciation of the word.

Theme Preferences

Toggle between Light and Dark modes for comfortable viewing in any environment.

Weather Units

Switch seamlessly between Metric (°C) and Imperial (°F) measurements.

Chat Management

Use the New Chat (pen and square) icon to clear your history and start a fresh conversation.
