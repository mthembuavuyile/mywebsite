Understanding Pyramid Schemes

Pyramid schemes rely on recruiting new members to pay returns to earlier investors. This unsustainable model eventually collapses, leaving most participants with significant losses. Key signs of a pyramid scheme include:

  • Emphasis on recruiting new participants
  • Promised high returns with little risk
  • Lack of a genuine product or service
  • Complex commission structure that prioritizes recruitment
Be aware: If it sounds too good to be true, it probably is!
