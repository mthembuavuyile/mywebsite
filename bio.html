About Me

Born in April 2005 and raised in KwaZulu-Natal province, South Africa, I'm also known as MR A CEO. I completed my high school education at Thornhill Christian College, where I developed a strong interest in science and technology.

Following my passion, I enrolled at Boston City Campus for a three-year diploma in systems development, where I gained extensive knowledge in software engineering principles and practices.

Beyond coding, I'm deeply interested in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and Bitcoin, which I view as the ultimate form of money. My entrepreneurial spirit led me to found Vylex Nexys, a company still in its early stages but already making a difference by developing websites for businesses in Durban, offering them competitive advantages online through search engine optimization and creating Google Business profiles.

I'm committed to using my technical skills for philanthropic purposes, creating solutions that not only solve problems but also contribute positively to society.
