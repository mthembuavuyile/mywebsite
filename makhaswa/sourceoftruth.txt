# **COMPANY OVERVIEW**

Makhaswa Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic civil engineering and construction company based in Pretoria, Gauteng, South Africa. Established in 2017, the company has built a strong reputation as a trusted partner in delivering large-scale infrastructure and building projects across both public and private sectors.

With a strong commitment to quality, efficiency, and reliability, Makhaswa Holdings consistently delivers projects on time and within budget while contributing to sustainable community development.

We don’t just construct buildings — we create lasting legacies. From concept to completion, every project is executed with precision, passion, and excellence.
