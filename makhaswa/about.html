Company Overview — Who we are, what we stand for, and how we deliver.

Who We Are

Makhaswa Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic civil engineering and construction company based in Pretoria, Gauteng. Founded in 2017, the company has established itself as a reliable partner in delivering large-scale infrastructure and building projects across both public and private sectors.

With a strong focus on quality, efficiency, and reliability, Makhaswa Holdings consistently delivers projects on time and within budget while contributing to sustainable community development.

"We don't just construct buildings — we create lasting legacies. From concept to completion, we bring your vision to life with precision, passion, and excellence."

Our Vision and Commitment

Our Vision

At Makhaswa Holdings, we aim to build long-lasting infrastructure that supports economic growth and uplifts communities. We strive to be a leading indigenous construction and infrastructure development company, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and community empowerment.

Our Journey

Established in 2017, Makhaswa Holdings is a private company that has grown into a reliable, multi-disciplined construction firm based in Moreleta Park, Pretoria.

2017
Makhaswa Holdings founded. Entry into residential and small-scale civil works in Gauteng.
2019
Awarded first major government infrastructure contract. Expanded plant & equipment fleet.
2022
Achieved Level 1 B-BBEE status with 135% procurement recognition.
2024
Secured trusted partner status with the City of Tshwane. CIDB Grade 8 CE PE achieved.
Present
Operating across civil, building, mechanical, electrical, and specialist works with active CIDB status.
