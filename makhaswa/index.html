About Our Company

Built to Last.
Engineered to Perform.

Founded in 2017, Makhaswa Holdings is a premier civil engineering and construction company based in the City of Tshwane, Gauteng. We are committed to delivering high-quality projects on time, within budget, and to the strictest safety standards.

Our dedicated team serves both private and public sector clients across South Africa. We don't just move earth; we lay the foundations for economic growth by combining modern engineering techniques with a robust, skilled workforce.
