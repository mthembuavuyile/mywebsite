About Us — Discover our roots, our vision for tomorrow, and the core values that drive our daily operations.

Who We Are

Makhaswa Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a private, 100% Black-owned, management-driven business. Established with a commitment to transforming the construction landscape, we have grown into a highly capable organization delivering large-scale infrastructure, commercial, and residential projects.

Our multidisciplinary approach allows us to manage end-to-end project lifecycles, ensuring safety, quality, and timely delivery. We pride ourselves on building lasting relationships with clients across both the public and private sectors.

Plant, Equipment & Safety

Plant & Equipment

Makhaswa Holdings owns and maintains a vast, state-of-the-art fleet of yellow plant and construction machinery. This internal capability reduces our reliance on third-party hires, ensuring project timelines are strictly adhered to.

Our fleet includes excavators, graders, TLBs, rollers, dump trucks, and specialized earth-moving equipment — all serviced and maintained by a dedicated in-house workshop.

Zero Harm Policy

Health and Safety is non-negotiable at Makhaswa. We strictly adhere to the Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA) and hold ISO 45001 certification. Our robust safety management systems protect our employees, sub-contractors, clients, and the public on every site.

Social Responsibility — We build communities as much as we build infrastructure.

Social Responsibility

Investment in Communities

Through our CSI initiatives, Makhaswa Holdings actively funds and builds local schools, community centers, and sports facilities in the regions we operate. We source materials locally and mandate minimum employment targets for local community members on every project site.

Training & Skills Development

We bridge the skills gap in the construction sector by providing bursaries to university engineering students and running comprehensive apprenticeship programs for artisans (bricklaying, plumbing, carpentry). Internal staff undergo continuous CPD training.
