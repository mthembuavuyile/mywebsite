Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries regarding our services, capabilities, and compliance standards.

Common Enquiries
What areas does Makhaswa Holdings operate in?

While our head office is located in Pretoria (City of Tshwane, Gauteng), Makhaswa Holdings operates across multiple provinces in South Africa, handling various large-scale infrastructure and building projects for both public and private sectors.

What are your CIDB grading capabilities?

We hold significant multi-disciplinary capabilities, including 8 CE PE (Civil Engineering), 6 GB PE (General Building), 8 ME PE (Mechanical Engineering), 7 EB PE (Electrical Engineering), and 7 SB PE (Specialist Building Works). This allows us to handle highly complex and large-scale infrastructure projects.

Are you registered with the NHBRC?

Yes. Makhaswa Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a registered and certified home builder with the NHBRC (Registration Number: 4000006217). We strictly adhere to the Housing Consumers Protection Measures Act to deliver durable and regulated residential structures.

How do I request a quote or submit a tender enquiry?

For official project enquiries and tender discussions, please visit our Contact page. You can submit your requirements via the structured enquiry form or contact our head office directly via phone or email.

What is your B-BBEE status?

Makhaswa Holdings is a proud Level 1 B-BBEE Contributor, with a 135% Procurement Recognition rating. We are deeply committed to broad-based empowerment and economic transformation in South Africa.
