SA Student Pass

A Digital Identity System for South African Universities

Prepared by: Avuyile Mthembu & Anele Immanuel Tembe

The Problem: The Failing Physical Card

  • Insecure: Easily lost, stolen, or duplicated, posing significant security risks.
  • Inconvenient: Forgetting a card means no access to libraries, exams, or transport.
  • Costly & Wasteful: Millions spent on plastic cards that harm the environment.
  • Fragmented: Dozens of different card designs lead to errors and fraud.

The Solution: SA Student Pass

A secure, digital-first student identity system. We replace physical cards with a single, dynamic QR code pass on a smartphone, universally recognized, forgery-proof, and it works offline.

Core Features & Technology

Digital Pass

Stored in Apple or Google Wallet, displaying student info and enrollment status.

Dynamic QR Code

Refreshes every 5 minutes, making screenshots and forgeries useless.

Account-Based Identity

Linked to the university email for instant recovery and deactivation on a new device.

Key Differentiators

Advanced Security

  • Dynamic Tokens: Time-bound QR codes prevent "replay attacks".
  • Signed Payloads: Each QR code proves enrollment.
  • Instant Revocation: Secure a lost phone via account login.
  • POPIA Compliance: Minimal data usage and universities retain control.

True Offline Functionality

  • On-Device Generation: QR codes are generated without needing internet.
  • Verification Cache: Scanners can validate passes without a live connection.
  • Reliable Access: Ensures the system works for all students, everywhere.

The Value Proposition

For Students

Instant access from their phone, no replacement fees, and offline reliability.

For Universities

Zero card production costs, fraud-proof verification, and reduced admin load.

For Service Providers

A single, universal standard for validation, eliminating fraud and manual inspection.

For the Environment

Eliminates plastic waste from PVC cards, contributing to a greener campus.
