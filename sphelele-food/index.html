Who We Are

Our Story

Sphelele Food is a restaurant born in Durban, inspired by the vibrant Nguni culture. We draw our culinary inspiration from the Zulu, Xhosa, Swati, and Ndebele traditions, with touches of Sotho and Tswana flavors.

We are dedicated to serving only authentic South African traditional food. Whether you are craving Ulusu, Hardbody Chicken, or Steamed Bread, we prepare it with the respect and love it deserves.
